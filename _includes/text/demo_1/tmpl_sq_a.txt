Jekyll, used by GitHub Pages, handles inclusion processing (etc)
by means of Liquid.
Unfortunately, some of the syntax used by Liquid and Handlebars
(eg, <tt>{&#123; ... &#125;}</tt>, <tt>{&#123;{ ... }&#125;}</tt>) collides.

(eg, <tt>{| ... |}</tt>, <tt>{|| ... ||}</tt>).
